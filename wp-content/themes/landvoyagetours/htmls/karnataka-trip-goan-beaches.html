Karnataka trip with Goan beaches

Day 1 : Arrival Goa

Today arrive at the Goa Airport where you will be received by our tour executive, who will introduce the driver. Later check in the hotel refresh and rest of the day is at leisure to enjoy the beautiful beaches and explore Old Goa. Overnight stay at Panjim Inn, Goa

Day 2 : Goa

Today you can spend in Goa beaches and enjoy the beauty of Indian beaches. Overnight stay at Panjim Inn, Goa

Day 3 –Goa - Badami

Morning after breakfast you will drive to Badami on reaching the hotel check in and relax the day at leisure. Overnight stay at The Heritage Resort, Badami

Day 4 : Badami- Aihole – Pattadakal – Badami

This day city tour of Badami visit Badami caves and the number of temples that are located near by the lake, The chain of cave temples in Badami are fabulous example of cave architecture next to Ajanta & Ellora here you can witness the full range of religious sects. Back in Badami, you are free to explore the town on your own. Overnight stay at The Heritage Resort, Badami

Day 5 :Badami – Hospet

Morning after breakfast drive to Hospet, on the way visit Aihole and Pattadakal that includes sightseeing of the famous Archeological museum, Virupaksha temple and Malikarjuna temple.. Aihole is where you will see the evolution of Hindu temple architecture from the early shrines of the Lad Khan Temple to more complex structures like that of the Meguti Temple. Pattadakal, once the second capital of the Badami Chalukyans dynasty, was also the playground of Hindu temple architects. After reaching the hotel check in and relax the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Malligi Hotel, Hospet

Day 6 : Hospet(Excursion to Hampi)

This day after breakfast drive to Hampi on a day excursion and visit Vithala temple, Virupaksha temple, Hazara Rama temple, Old Palace temple and Lakshmi Narasimha temple. The 25 Sq Kms of the ruins of Hampi will overwhelm you with its Glory of the bygone era of the Vijayanagara Kingdom. Overnight stay at Malligi Hotel, Hospet

Day 7 : Hospet – Hassan (Excursion to Belur & Halebid)

Today will have a long trip towards Hassan which will be a very hectic trip due to the very bad road conditions, so prepare yourself both mentally and physically for a long day in road. After your arrival in Hassan relax yourself in your hotel in Hassan. Overnight stay at Hoysala Village, Hassan

Day 8: Hassan – Mysore

Today morning visit the Belur & Halebid temples, which are places of architectural wonders, where the sightseeing includes Chennakeshava temple, Hoysaleshwara temple, Jain temples and have a quick glimpse at Chennigaraya, Veeranarayana, Sridevi, and Bhoodevi temples which are all located in the same complex. Later drive towards Mysore. Overnight stay at Royal Orchid Metropole, Mysore

Day 9: Mysore – Sightseeing

Today morning travel to the village of Somnathpur located on the banks of the Kaveri River. The village is famous for its beautiful and well preserved Keshava temple. The temple is enclosed in a walled courtyard and is built on an elevated star shaped platform. The temple is a fine example of a temple built by the Hoysalas and has the trademark intricate sculpture work. Later you can continue your tour by returning to Mysore for further sightseeing. You will be enthralled by Mysore palace with its displays of art from all around the world. Located on top of Chamundi Hill the tour continues by a visit to The Chamundeeswari temple. It houses an impressive sculpture of a bull carved out of a single granite rock which is 4.57 meters in height and 6.10 meters in length. Later finish your tour by a visit to the Devaraja Market with all its colorful flowers, fruits, and vegetables. Overnight stay at Royal Orchid Metropole, Mysore

Day 10 : Mysore – Ooty

Today morning drive to the famous hill station Ooty, On reaching Ooty the "Queen of hill stations" a land of dreams and a picturesque hill station, make a visit to Botanical garden, Sim's park and Dodabetta peak and evening enjoy at the Ooty lake. Overnight stay at Sullivan Court, Ooty

Day 11 : Ooty – Coimbatore Departurey
