Malaysia & Singapore Compo package

Day 1 Arrival in Kuala Lumpur / Kuala Lumpur Night Tour

Welcome to Malaysia - Upon arrival, check in to the hotel and spend some time at leisure.Later in Evening, experience an intimate evening on Kuala Night tour.We take a drive to Chinatown, an open-air "bazaar" bustling with brightly lit and loud voices of vendors. We visit the 100 year’s old Sri Mahamariamman temple, which is located in Chinatown, the oldest Hindu temple in the city with intricate and elaborative decorations on Hindu deities.Then drive passes the streets of Kuala Lumpur, which is beautifully illuminated befitting its names as Garden City of Lights, Kuala Lumpur Tower observation deck and photo stop at one of the largest towers in the world. Overnight stay at Hotel

Day 2 Day at Leisure(Sunway lagoon tour – Optional)

After breakfast at Hotel, Day free for leisure or shopping or You can take the optinal tour of sunway lagoon resort to enjoy your day at the wet and dry park (7 hours stay at sun way lagoon) . on your way back, you will be taken to an Indian restaurant or hotel to enjoy dinner. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 3 Kualalumpur – Genting Highland

After breakfast at Hotel you will be picked up from your hotel lobby to be transferd to Genting highland (oneway cable car ride included), it is a magnificent city perched on a hilltop 2000 metres above sea level offers a cool respite from the hustle & bustle of the city capital. Crisp invigorating mountain air, majestic sceneries and lush green tropical rainforests shrouded in a veil of mist come together to create a pleasant surprise and a welcome relief from urban sights & sounds. On arrival check in and relax for the rest of the day, if you are of the eligible age a visit to the casinos is a must! Overnight stay at Hotel

Day 4 Malaysia to Singapore & evening Night safari

Enjoy early breakfast and head on your way to the World’s Greatest City, Singapore! Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel. Check-in to your hotel, freshen up and spend the rest of the day at leisure. We recommend you kick-start your Singapore adventure with the Night Safari (on your own). Overnight stay Hotel

Day 5 Singapore Half-Day City Tour

After enjoying a scrumptious breakfast at the hotel, gear up for an exciting half-day city tour of Singapore, You will begin the tour with Gardens by the Bay ’ (rated as the Top 10 Indoor Gardens Of The World), an award winning attraction which is renowned worldwide for its beauty, serenity and pure innovation. It will truly prove to be an unforgettable experience.Next, you will get to see the Merlion Statue – one of the hallmarks of Singapore. After that, you will stop at China Town where you can shop for reasonably priced trinkets and souvenirs. After that, we will take you to an amazing Chocolate Factory and Jewellery Shop. The tour will commence at Mt. Faber and Little India. Overnight stay at Hotel

Day 6 Sentosa Island Tour

The morning is free to relax. In the evening, you can choose to spend time at Asia’s largest playground, Sentosa Island at an additional cost, As the prime tourist destination in Singapore, Sentosa Island Resort offers a variety of attractions,museums and other facilities to endow a whole range of breathless experience. The ticket includes admission to One Way Cable Car Ride, Dolphin Lagoon, Under WaterWorld and Wings of Time. Overnight Stay at Hotel

Day 7 Departure For India

After breakfast, check out of the hotel.Later, get transferred to the airport to board your return flight to India.
