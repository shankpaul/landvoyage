Incredible North India Trip

Day 1: Delhi Arrival

Arrive at the capital city of India called Delhi by RJ0192 at 5am where you will be received by our company representative and transfer to the hotel. Later guided sightseeing tour of Old Delhi and enjoy a cycle rickshaw ride through the narrow, busy and colorful streets of Chandini Chowk which will amaze you at every second. Overnight stay at Hotel Hans

Day 2: Delhi – Agra (200kms/4hrs)

After breakfast do the sightseeing tour of New Delhi and later drive to Agra which attracts thousands of tourists from all over the world to see one of the Seven Wonders of the World called Taj Mahal. On reaching Agra, check into the hotel and relax for the day. Overnight stay at Hotel Grand Imperial –

Day 3: Agra

After breakfast start the guided tour of the great Taj Mahal, Agra Fort and Itmud – u – Daulla’s tomb popularly known as Baby Taj, later day free for own activities. Overnight stay at Grand Imperial

Day 4: Agra – Fatehpur Sikri – Jaipur (260kms/6hrs)

After breakfast drive to Jaipur and enroute stop to visit the ruins of great Mughal dynasty called Fatehpur Sikri. On reaching Jaipur, check into the hotel and relax for the day. Overnight stay at Hotel Trident

Day 5: Jaipur

After breakfast guided visit to Amber Fort located in the outskirts of Jaipur city. Then visit City Palace Museum, Hawa Mahal (Palace of Wind) and Jantar Mantar (ancient scientific observatory). Then visit a rug factory and see the ladies in the intricate work or visit a gem factory and see the cutting and polishing work involved. Later free time to walk around and explore the colorful handicraft and textile market. Overnight stay at Trident.

Day 6: Jaipur – Shahpura (220kms/3.5hrs)

After breakfast drive to Shahpura and check into the lovely homestay called Shahpura Bagh. This is a great place to relax during your trip and you have the option for birding tour and nature walk. Overnight stay at Shahpura Bagh Heritage Homestay.

Day 7: Shahpura - Udaipur (220kms/4hrs)

After breakfast drive to the romantic city of Udaipur also referred as Venice of the East. The beauty of Udaipur lies in its seven lakes which surrounds the city. On reaching Udaipur, check into the hotel and After guided visit to Jagdish temple and City Palace, in the evening enjoy boat ride to explore the beauty of Lake Pichola. Overnight stay at Trident

Day 8: Udaipur – Jodhpur (280kms/5hrs)

After breakfast drive to Jodhpur which is referred as Blue City. On arrival check into the hotel and After start the full day guided tour visiting Clock tower, Jaswant Thada Cenotaph, Mehrangarh Fort and Umaid Bhavan Palace Museum, later free time for own activities. Overnight stay at Ajit Bhawan

Day 9: Jodhpur – Jaisalmer (330kms/6hrs)

After breakfast drive to Jaisalmer and on arrival check into the hotel and After breakfast guided tour of Gadsisar Lake, Jaisalmer Fort which is one of oldest living fort in the world. There are some magnificent Jain temples inside the fort. Then visit the beautifully carved and painted Nathmalji Ki Haveli, Salim Singh’s Haveli and Patwon Ki Haveli. Later free time to explore the local market. Overnight stay at Rawal Kot

Day 10: Jaisalmer – Manvar (170kms/3hrs) – Jodhpur (120kms/2hrs) – Mumbai (by flight)

After breakfast drive to a small village called Manvar and After drive back to Jodhpur to board the Jet Airways flight 9W 2544 at 13.30hrs and you shall reach Mumbai at 15.25hrs.
